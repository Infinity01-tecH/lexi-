Swipe through the core experiences. Each panel shows the kind of motion the product should feel like: alive, guided, and hard to ignore.

1. AI Learning Core

AI Learning Core

Lexi starts by listening to your goal, then opens a guided AI lesson that feels like a real tutor. It explains grammar in plain words, catches mistakes instantly, and rewrites your sentence with clear examples. Every reply adapts to your level, so beginners stay calm while advanced learners keep moving fast.

2. Speaking & Communication

Live voice, live response

When you reach voice mode, the mic goes live and the lesson becomes conversation practice. Lexi listens in real time, detects pronunciation gaps, and answers instantly so the flow never breaks. You can repeat a phrase, slow it down, and try again until your rhythm sounds natural and confident in everyday speech.

3. Real-Life Practice

Practice in living scenes

Lexi then places you inside situations like airports, interviews, and restaurants, where every line has context. You respond as if the moment is happening now, not in a textbook. If you hesitate, Lexi gives soft hints and lets you continue the exchange, helping you build real conversational reflexes before real-world pressure appears.

4. Smart Learning System

Memory that follows you

Behind the scenes, Lexi tracks what you forget and what you master. Hard words return sooner, easy words step back, and your review queue changes daily without manual setup. That spacing logic keeps memory strong while saving time, so you are always practicing the right thing at the right moment.

5. Immersive Content

Watch, tap, understand

With immersive media, you learn from short videos, captions, and native phrasing in one screen. Tap any word to see meaning, tone, and usage without leaving the lesson. Instead of memorizing isolated terms, you absorb language through context, which makes comprehension faster and helps you remember expressions naturally in conversation.

6. Advanced Input

Understand deeper context

Advanced input mode lets you open dense content like articles, books, and scripts while Lexi stays beside you as a smart explainer. Select a phrase and get a quick meaning, grammar breakdown, and better alternatives. This turns difficult material into guided practice so your reading depth grows alongside speaking confidence.

7. Micro Learning

Five minutes still counts

Even short sessions create visible progress with Lexi micro learning. In five focused minutes, you review key words, complete one speaking prompt, and lock in a daily streak. The system rewards consistency over marathon study, so language growth stays realistic for busy days while momentum keeps building across the week.
